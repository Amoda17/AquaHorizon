JOIN US!

Join us in making a difference! Participate in our ocean sustainability projects.

Beach Cleanup

Join efforts to remove plastic waste from coastal areas.

Date:March 23 2025

Location: Crystal Bay Beach, Main Entrance

Time: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM

What to Bring: Gloves, reusable water bottle, sunscreen, and a hat.

Plan: Participants will be divided into teams to collect plastic waste, glass, and other debris along the shoreline. Refreshments will be provided.

Marine Pollution Awareness Program

Help protect marine species and restore coral reefs.

Date: April 5, 2025

Location: Ocean Conservation Center,Colombo, Main Auditorium

Time: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

What to Expect: Join us for an educational event featuring presentations, documentaries, and discussions about the impact of marine pollution and how we can take action to protect our oceans.

Topics Covered: Plastic waste, oil spills, chemical pollutants, and their effects on marine ecosystems and human health.

Why Attend? This program will help raise awareness about the urgent need to tackle marine pollution and give you the tools to make a positive change in your community.

Donation for Coral Reef Protection

Goal: Help protect and restore coral reefs around the world through your generous donations.

Donation Amount: You can choose any amount to donate to coral protection companies working towards preserving marine ecosystems.

How Your Donation Helps: Your contributions will fund coral reef restoration projects, support research, and aid in reducing threats like pollution and climate change.

Why Donate? Coral reefs are vital to marine biodiversity, and they provide shelter to countless marine species. Your support is crucial in ensuring they thrive for future generations!
